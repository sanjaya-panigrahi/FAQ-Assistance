# MyTechStore - Frequently Asked Questions

## Products and Availability

1. **What products do you sell?**
   We sell laptops, gaming PCs, smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, wearables, cameras, printers, routers, smart home devices, audio systems, accessories, and enterprise electronics.

2. **Do you sell refurbished products?**
   Yes. Refurbished devices are certified, tested, and include a minimum 6-month warranty.

3. **How do I check if a product is in stock?**
   Product pages display real-time inventory for online stock and branch-level availability.

4. **Can I pre-order upcoming devices?**
   Yes, selected products support pre-orders with expected shipping windows shown before checkout.

## Pricing and Payments

5. **Do prices include tax?**
   Prices shown on product pages are pre-tax. Final tax is calculated during checkout based on shipping address.

6. **Do you offer price matching?**
   We offer price matching for identical in-stock products from approved retailers within 7 days of purchase.

7. **Which payment methods are accepted?**
   We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, EMI, and major digital wallets.

8. **Do you provide EMI options?**
   Yes. EMI is available for eligible banks and cards on orders above $150.

## Shipping and Delivery

9. **How long does delivery take?**
   Standard shipping: 3-5 business days. Express shipping: 1-2 business days in metro areas.

10. **Do you ship internationally?**
    Currently we ship within India, Singapore, UAE, and selected EU regions.

11. **How can I track my order?**
    You can track through My Orders in your account or with the tracking link sent via email and SMS.

## Returns and Warranty

12. **What is your return policy?**
    Returns are accepted within 10 days for unopened items and within 7 days for defective items.

13. **How does warranty support work?**
    Warranty terms vary by brand and product category. Most products include 1-year manufacturer warranty.

14. **Can I buy extended warranty?**
    Yes, extended warranty and accidental damage plans are available for many products.

## Branches and Support

15. **Where are your branches located?**
    Major branches: Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata, Dubai, and Singapore.

16. **Do you offer in-store pickup?**
    Yes. Same-day pickup is available for selected items at participating branches.

17. **What are customer support hours?**
    24x7 chatbot support; live agent support from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM IST.

18. **How can business customers contact sales?**
    Enterprise sales can be reached at enterprise@mytechstore.example.

## Orders and Checkout

19. **Can I place an order without creating an account?**
   Yes. Guest checkout is available for most orders, though creating an account makes tracking and returns easier.

20. **How do I apply a coupon code?**
   Enter the coupon code in the promo field during checkout and click Apply before completing payment.

21. **Why did my payment fail?**
   Payment can fail because of bank authorization issues, incorrect card details, insufficient balance, or a session timeout.

22. **Will I receive an order confirmation email?**
   Yes. An order confirmation email and SMS are sent once payment is successfully processed.

23. **Can I change the shipping address after placing an order?**
   Address changes are possible only before the order moves to packed or shipped status.

24. **Can I modify items in my order after payment?**
   Item changes are not guaranteed after payment. In most cases you will need to cancel and place a new order.

25. **How do I cancel my order?**
   You can cancel eligible orders from My Orders before they are shipped, or contact support for help.

26. **Will I be charged immediately after placing the order?**
   Yes. The payment method is charged or authorized at checkout depending on the payment provider.

27. **Can I save multiple delivery addresses in my account?**
   Yes. Logged-in users can save and manage multiple shipping addresses in their account settings.

28. **Why was my order automatically cancelled?**
   Orders may be auto-cancelled due to payment failure, stock unavailability, fraud screening, or verification issues.

## Shipping and Delivery Details

29. **Do you offer same-day delivery?**
   Same-day delivery is available for selected products in eligible city zones when ordered before the daily cutoff time.

30. **What is the cutoff time for express delivery?**
   Express delivery cutoff times vary by city, but most metro orders placed before 2:00 PM are eligible for next-day delivery.

31. **Do you charge shipping fees?**
   Shipping fees depend on the item category, location, shipping speed, and current promotions.

32. **Is free shipping available?**
   Free shipping is offered on selected products and promotional campaigns that meet minimum cart value conditions.

33. **How are bulky items delivered?**
   Large appliances, TVs, and oversized products are delivered through specialized logistics partners with scheduled delivery slots.

34. **Can I choose a delivery time slot?**
   Delivery slot selection is available in supported cities and for eligible product categories.

35. **What happens if I miss my delivery?**
   The courier will usually attempt redelivery or contact you to reschedule depending on the shipment type.

36. **Do you deliver on weekends and holidays?**
   Weekend delivery is available in many regions, while holiday delivery depends on local logistics availability.

37. **Can I inspect the package at delivery?**
   You may inspect the outer packaging for visible damage before accepting delivery, subject to courier policy.

38. **What should I do if my package arrives damaged?**
   Take photos of the package, refuse delivery if severely damaged when possible, and contact support immediately.

## Returns, Refunds, and Replacements

39. **How long does a refund take to process?**
   Refunds are typically processed within 5 to 7 business days after the returned item passes inspection.

40. **Will the refund go back to the original payment method?**
   Yes. Refunds are usually issued to the original payment method unless store credit is selected where available.

41. **Can I request a replacement instead of a refund?**
   Yes. Replacements are available for eligible defective, damaged, or wrong-item deliveries subject to stock availability.

42. **Are software products returnable?**
   Download codes, activated software, and digital subscriptions are generally non-returnable unless legally required.

43. **Can opened accessories be returned?**
   Opened accessories may be returnable only if defective, damaged on arrival, or covered under a specific return exception.

44. **Do you charge a restocking fee?**
   Restocking fees are not charged on standard returns, but certain special-order items may have separate conditions.

45. **How do I track my return request?**
   You can monitor return status from My Orders or through updates sent by email and SMS.

46. **What if I receive the wrong item?**
   Report it within the return window with photos of the received product and packaging for faster resolution.

47. **What items are non-returnable?**
   Non-returnable items may include activated software, gift cards, hygiene-sensitive items, and clearance products marked final sale.

48. **Can I return a product purchased during a sale?**
   Sale items generally follow the standard return policy unless specifically marked non-returnable or final sale.

## Warranty and Protection Plans

49. **What does the manufacturer warranty cover?**
   Manufacturer warranty typically covers hardware defects and manufacturing faults under normal usage conditions.

50. **What is not covered under warranty?**
   Warranty usually excludes accidental damage, liquid damage, misuse, unauthorized repairs, and cosmetic wear.

51. **How do I claim warranty service?**
   Submit the invoice, serial number, and issue details through support or visit an authorized service center.

52. **Do I need the original invoice for warranty claims?**
   Yes. The original invoice or a valid order receipt is generally required to verify warranty eligibility.

53. **Can I buy accidental damage protection?**
   Yes. Accidental damage plans are available on many laptops, smartphones, tablets, and premium electronics.

54. **When does extended warranty begin?**
   Extended warranty usually starts after the standard manufacturer warranty period ends.

55. **Can protection plans be transferred to another owner?**
   Transferability depends on the provider and plan terms. Some plans are transferable with proof of purchase.

56. **How quickly are warranty repairs completed?**
   Repair timelines vary by brand and part availability, but most standard repairs are completed within 7 to 14 business days.

57. **Do refurbished products include warranty?**
   Yes. Certified refurbished products include a minimum 6-month warranty unless otherwise stated.

58. **Can I extend warranty after purchase?**
   In many cases, warranty extension can be added within a limited period after product purchase or activation.
